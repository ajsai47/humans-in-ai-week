# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Humans in AI Week!

## How to contribute

### For chapter leads

This repository is your toolkit. If you have suggestions for improving any of the materials:

1. Open an [Issue](https://github.com/ajsai47/humans-in-ai-week/issues) describing what you'd like to change
2. Or submit a Pull Request with your proposed changes

### For community members

- **Share feedback** from your local event in [Discussions](https://github.com/ajsai47/humans-in-ai-week/discussions)
- **Translate materials** into your local language (open an issue to coordinate)
- **Suggest discussion questions** for existing or new tracks
- **Share event photos and recaps** that can inspire other chapters

### For partners and sponsors

Contact aj@aicollective.com for partnership inquiries.

## What makes a good contribution

- **Practical improvements** to existing guides, templates, or copy
- **Translations** of materials into other languages
- **New discussion questions** grounded in real experiences
- **Event format ideas** that have been tested at real events
- **Bug fixes** (typos, broken links, outdated information)

## What we'll likely decline

- Major structural changes without prior discussion
- Content that contradicts the [Master Messaging Kit](./global/messaging-kit.md)
- Promotional or commercial content
- Materials that haven't been tested at a real event

## Discussions

Use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/ajsai47/humans-in-ai-week/discussions) for:

- Questions about hosting an event
- Sharing what worked at your event
- Connecting with other chapter leads
- Suggesting improvements to this toolkit

## Code of conduct

All contributions are subject to our [Code of Conduct](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
