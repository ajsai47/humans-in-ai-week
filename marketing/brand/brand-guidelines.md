## Usage rules

1. Do not alter the HAIW logo (stretch, recolor, add effects)
2. Always maintain clear space around the logo
3. When co-branding with a sponsor, HAIW logo should be equal size or larger
4. Use the provided Canva templates for social graphics — don't design from scratch unless approved
