# Flagship Conferences

In addition to the 200+ free chapter meetups happening globally, HAIW runs **flagship conferences** in major hub cities — full-day, ticketed, 1,000–2,000 person events featuring speakers from AIC's global partner network.

These are the marquee events of the week. The chapter meetups are the conversation. The flagships are the stage.

---

## Overview

| Parameter | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Format** | Full-day conference (9 AM – 6 PM) + evening reception |
| **Scale** | 1,000–2,000 attendees per city |
| **Tickets** | $49–$199 (tiered pricing) |
| **Cities** | San Francisco, New York, Washington DC (+ additional TBD) |
| **Dates** | During HAIW week, June 1–7, 2026 |
| **Organized by** | AIC central team (not chapter leads) |

## How flagships differ from chapter meetups

| | Chapter meetups | Flagship conferences |
|---|---|---|
| **Scale** | 50–200 people | 1,000–2,000 people |
| **Duration** | 2–3 hours (evening) | Full day (9 AM – 6 PM) |
| **Format** | Panel, roundtable, discussion | Keynotes, panels, demos, networking |
| **Cost** | Free | $49–$199 |
| **Speakers** | Local voices | AIC partner network — CxOs, researchers, founders |
| **Organized by** | Chapter leads | AIC central team |
| **Sponsorship** | Local sponsors ($500–$1,500) | National/global sponsors ($10K–$100K+) |
| **Audience** | General public, community | Industry leaders, builders, press, investors |
| **Cities** | 200+ globally | 3–5 major hubs |
