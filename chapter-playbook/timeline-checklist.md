# 10-Week Planning Timeline

Count backwards from your event date (June 1–7, 2026).

## Week 10 (March 24–28)

- [ ] Confirm you're hosting an event — notify AIC chapter ops
- [ ] Read this entire playbook
- [ ] Choose your event format (panel, roundtable, hybrid, workshop)
- [ ] Start venue scouting (aim for 3 options)

## Week 9 (March 31 – April 4)

- [ ] Choose your [discussion track(s)](../content/discussion-guides/)
- [ ] Begin local sponsor outreach using the [Local Sponsor Pitch](../sponsorship/local-sponsor-pitch.md)
- [ ] Begin community partner outreach using the [Community Partner Kit](../sponsorship/local-community-partner-kit.md)
- [ ] Identify 4–6 potential panelists/facilitators

## Week 8 (April 7–11)

- [ ] Confirm venue (signed agreement or written confirmation)
- [ ] Send speaker invitations using the [Speaker Invite Template](../marketing/email-templates/speaker-invite.md)
- [ ] Register your event on Luma
- [ ] Submit your Luma link to AIC for the global calendar

## Week 7 (April 14–18)

- [ ] Confirm at least 1 local sponsor
- [ ] Confirm 2–4 panelists/facilitators
- [ ] Draft your [Run of Show](./run-of-show-template.md)

## Week 6 (April 21–25)

- [ ] Begin promoting — post first announcement using [social copy](../marketing/social-copy/)
- [ ] Share event link with your chapter mailing list
- [ ] Customize the [Canva event square template](../marketing/) for your city

## Week 5 (April 28 – May 2)

- [ ] Second wave of social promotion
- [ ] Send personal invites to top local contacts
- [ ] Confirm catering/food details with sponsor or venue

## Week 4 (May 5–9)

- [ ] Check registration numbers — if below target, increase promotion
- [ ] Brief panelists on discussion guide and format
- [ ] Confirm A/V setup with venue

## Week 3 (May 12–16)

- [ ] Send reminder email to all registrants
- [ ] Post countdown content on social
- [ ] Finalize day-of logistics (parking, access, signage)

## Week 2 (May 19–23)

- [ ] Final promotion push
- [ ] Prepare day-of materials (name tags, signage, printed guides if needed)
- [ ] Do a dry run of the Run of Show with your team

## Week 1 (May 26–30)

- [ ] Send final reminder to registrants (48 hours before)
- [ ] Confirm all panelists and facilitators
- [ ] Charge devices, test equipment
- [ ] Rest — you're ready

## Event Week (June 1–7)

- [ ] Execute your event
- [ ] Take photos and capture quotes
- [ ] Post on social with **#HumansInAIWeek**
- [ ] Submit [Post-Event Report](./post-event-report-template.md) within 48 hours
