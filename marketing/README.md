## How to promote your event

### Timeline

| When | What |
|------|------|
| **6–8 weeks out** | Announce: first LinkedIn/X post, email to your chapter list |
| **4 weeks out** | Push: second round of social, personal invites to key people |
| **2 weeks out** | Countdown: "2 weeks until HAIW in [city]" content |
| **1 week out** | Urgency: "Last chance to RSVP" — share attendee count |
| **Day of** | Hype: "Tonight. [City]. Join us." + event details |
| **Day after** | Recap: Photos, quotes, thank-yous |
