# Ticket Tiers

Pricing strategy for flagship HAIW conferences.

---

## Pricing principles

1. **Accessible but not free.** Tickets signal commitment and filter for serious attendees. But price shouldn't exclude students, nonprofits, or underrepresented groups.
2. **Early bird rewards action.** People who commit early get the best price.
3. **VIP creates a premium experience** — not just better seats, but access (dinner, speakers, networking).
4. **Comps are strategic.** Speakers, press, partners, and select community leaders get complimentary access.

---

## Ticket tiers

| Tier | Price | Includes | Availability |
|------|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Super Early Bird** | $49 | General admission — all main stage + breakouts | First 200 tickets or until 8 weeks before event |
| **Early Bird** | $79 | General admission — all main stage + breakouts | Until 4 weeks before event |
| **General Admission** | $129 | All main stage + breakouts + networking reception | Until sold out |
| **Student / Nonprofit** | $49 | General admission (requires .edu or nonprofit email, approval required) | Limited to 10% of capacity |
| **Group Pass (5+)** | $99/each | General admission, billed together | Minimum 5 tickets |
| **VIP** | $199 | Reserved seating, VIP lounge, afterparty access, speaker meet & greet | Limited to 100 per city |
| **VIP Dinner** | $499–$999 | Everything in VIP + intimate dinner with select speakers (20–40 seats) | Invite-only, approval required |

## Comp / scholarship tickets

| Category | # per city | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| Speakers | 30–40 | All speakers get complimentary access |
| Press / media | 10–20 | Credentialed journalists, bloggers, podcasters |
| Community partners | 20–30 | 2–5 per partner org |
| AIC chapter leads | 10–20 | Chapter leads from the host city + nearby cities |
| Scholarship | 30–50 | Application-based, for people who can't afford a ticket |
| Sponsors | Per agreement | Typically 5–20 per sponsor tier |

## Ticket platform

Use **Luma** for consistency with chapter meetups and the global HAIW calendar. Luma supports:
- Multiple ticket tiers
- Approval-required tickets (for student/VIP dinner)
- Access codes for comps and partner allocations
- Waitlists
- Attendee management

Alternative: Eventbrite or Tito if Luma limitations arise at 1,500+ scale.

---

## Refund policy

| Timing | Refund |
|--------|--------|
| 30+ days before event | Full refund |
| 14–30 days before | 50% refund |
| Under 14 days | No refund (transfer to another person allowed) |
| Day of no-show | No refund |
