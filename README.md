## What is Humans in AI Week?

Humans in AI Week is a global week of simultaneous public events hosted by every [AI Collective](https://aicollective.com) chapter in the world. 200+ cities. 50+ countries. The same conversation, everywhere.

Not a conference. Not a protest. A convening — for everyone navigating AI in 2026, which is everyone.

**The question we are all gathering to ask: where are we going with AI, and who gets a say?**

### Why now

0.01% of the population is building a technology that will govern the other 99.99%. AI is moving faster than humanity's ability to orient around it. Most people feel the urgency and have nowhere to go with it.

AIC has run 1,000+ events over three years. We know how to get humans in rooms together. This is that formula applied globally, simultaneously, for the first time.

### Event parameters

| Parameter | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Dates** | June 1–7, 2026 |
| **Format** | Chapter-led: panel, roundtable, or open discussion. 2–3 hours. |
| **Scale** | 200+ cities, 50+ countries, 50–200 attendees per event |
| **Virtual** | Livestream component for chapters without a local venue |
| **Website** | [humansinaiweek.com](https://humansinaiweek.com) |

2. Pick a [discussion track](./content/discussion-guides/) (or combine multiple)
3. Secure a [local sponsor](./sponsorship/local-sponsor-pitch.md) and/or [community partner](./sponsorship/local-community-partner-kit.md)

7. Submit your [post-event report](./chapter-playbook/post-event-report-template.md) within 48 hours

Every event centers on one or more of these six tracks. Pick what resonates with your community.

| Track | Description | Guide |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| What It Means to Be Human in the AI Era | Identity, creativity, meaning, and what makes us irreplaceable | [Guide](./content/discussion-guides/track-1-human-in-ai-era.md) |
| AI and the Future of Work | Jobs, skills, automation, and economic transition | [Guide](./content/discussion-guides/track-2-future-of-work.md) |
