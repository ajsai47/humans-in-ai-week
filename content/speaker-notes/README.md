# Speaker Notes

Optional framing slides and talking points for panelists and keynote speakers.

> **Note**: HAIW events are conversations, not presentations. These materials are for speakers who want background context — not for reading from on stage.

## Coming soon

Slide decks and one-pagers for each discussion track will be added here as they're finalized.

If you're a panelist and want to prepare, the best thing you can do is:
1. Read the [discussion guide](../discussion-guides/) for your track
2. Think of 2–3 personal stories or examples related to the questions
3. Have a point of view — but hold it loosely

The audience came to hear you think out loud, not deliver a TED talk.
