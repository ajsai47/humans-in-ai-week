# Global Community Partner Kit

> **Google Doc version**: [Global Community Partner Kit (G-01)](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1wZ7Eyt-ou-ycK55Qg7toaDUDKXdH6O_P/edit)

Recruitment playbook for global community organizations as official HAIW Community Partners.

---

## Goal

20–50 confirmed global community partners before June 1, 2026.

## What is a global community partner?

An organization with a large audience (newsletter, community, network) that promotes HAIW to their members. In return, they get co-branding, recognition, and association with the initiative.

## Target tiers

### Tier 1: AI newsletters and media (highest priority)

- The Rundown AI
- TLDR AI
- Ben's Bites
- AlphaSignal
- FutureTools
- AI Breakfast
- Superhuman Newsletter

**Ask:** Dedicated send or featured segment during HAIW week.

### Tier 2: Community platforms

- Slack/Discord communities (MLOps Community, AI Engineers, etc.)
- LinkedIn groups with 10K+ members
- Substack writers covering AI

**Ask:** Share HAIW with their community. Cross-promote.

### Tier 3: Professional and academic networks

- Professional associations (IEEE, ACM local chapters)
- University AI clubs and departments
- Civic tech organizations

**Ask:** Co-host a local event or promote to their network.

## Partner onboarding (after they say yes)

1. Confirm partnership via email
2. Collect their logo (PNG/SVG)
3. Add to humansinaiweek.com partner section
4. Send them a partner badge graphic (P-04)
5. Share the marketing copy pack so they can customize
6. Confirm promotion timing (ideally during May or event week)
