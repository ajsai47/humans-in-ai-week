## Event name

**Humans in AI Week**

Not "Human AI Week." Not "AI Week." Not "The Human Era." Always "Humans in AI Week" or "HAIW" for shorthand.

## One-liner

> The largest coordinated civilian conversation about AI ever organized.

## Core problem

0.01% of the population is building a technology that will govern the other 99.99%. AI is moving faster than humanity's ability to orient around it. Most people feel the urgency and have nowhere to go with it.

## What it is

A global week of simultaneous public events hosted by every AI Collective chapter in the world. June 1–7, 2026. 200+ cities. 50+ countries. One week. The same conversation.

## What it is NOT

- Not a conference
- Not a protest
- Not a webinar
- Not a product launch
- Not a recruitment event

It is a **convening** — for everyone navigating AI in 2026, which is everyone.

## The question

> Where are we going with AI, and who gets a say?

## 5 core messages

1. **The conversation about AI should include everyone, not just the people building it.**
2. **AI is moving faster than humanity's ability to orient around it. We need to get in the room.**
3. **This is the largest coordinated civilian conversation about AI ever organized.**
4. **200+ cities. 50+ countries. One week. The same conversation.**
5. **Not a conference. A convening.**

## Tone guide

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Urgent but not alarmist | Don't doom-monger |
| Inclusive — "this is for everyone" | Don't gatekeep — no jargon, no prerequisites |
| Grounded — real examples, real stakes | Don't be abstract or academic |
| Hopeful — agency, not helplessness | Don't be cynical |
| Direct — short sentences, clear CTAs | Don't overwrite or hedge |

## Emotional arc

**Awareness** → "This is happening. AI is changing everything."
**Urgency** → "We don't have unlimited time to figure this out."
**Invitation** → "There's a room for you. This week. In your city."
**Agency** → "Your voice matters. Show up."

## CTA hierarchy

1. **RSVP for your local event** (primary — always the first CTA)
2. **Host an event in your city** (for cities without a chapter)
3. **Become a community partner** (for organizations)
4. **Become a sponsor** (for businesses)
5. **Share with your network** (for everyone)
