# Outreach Templates

Copy-paste templates for every outreach scenario. Customize the bracketed fields.

---

## Speaker invitation

> **Subject:** Speak at Humans in AI Week — [City], June [date]
>
> Hi [Name],
>
> I'm [your name], chapter lead for The AI Collective in [city]. I'm putting together a panel for Humans in AI Week — a global event happening in 200+ cities during June 1–7.
>
> Our event in [city] is on **[date]** at **[venue]**, and we're expecting **[X]** attendees. The discussion topic is **[track name — e.g., "AI and the Future of Work"]**.
>
> I'd love to have you on the panel. Here's what it involves:
> - A 60–75 minute moderated discussion with 2–3 other panelists
> - No slides or prep required — just come ready to share your perspective
> - I'll send discussion questions in advance so nothing catches you off guard
>
> [One sentence on why you're asking them specifically — e.g., "Your work on AI adoption at [company] makes you exactly the kind of voice we need in this conversation."]
>
> Would you be open to joining? Happy to share more details.
>
> Best,
> [Your name]
