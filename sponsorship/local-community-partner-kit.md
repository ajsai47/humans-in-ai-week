# Local Community Partner Kit

> **Google Doc version**: [Local Community Partner Kit (L-03)](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1rogPLOYBDN4UqBmlMr5KKNCi_DSPeFrJ/edit)

Use this to recruit local organizations as community co-hosts for your HAIW event.

---

## What is a community partner?

A community partner is a local organization that helps promote and co-hosts your HAIW event. Think: universities, libraries, professional associations, civic groups, coworking spaces, local tech meetups.

They're not sponsors (no money changes hands). They're amplifiers — they bring their audience and credibility, you bring the event.

### What they get

- 5 complimentary tickets (reserved seats)
- Co-branding on event page and materials
- Recognition during the event
- Association with a global initiative

### What you get

- Access to their audience and mailing list
- Credibility boost (especially with universities and civic organizations)
- Potential venue (many partners have event space)
- Broader, more diverse attendance

## Talking points (if they ask questions)

**"What's the time commitment?"**
> Minimal. Share one social post or email with your community. Show up if you can. That's it.

**"We're not an AI organization."**
> That's the point. This conversation is for everyone navigating AI, not just people building it. Your community is exactly who needs to be in the room.

**"We don't have budget for this."**
> No budget needed. This is a cross-promotion partnership — your audience, our event, mutual benefit.
