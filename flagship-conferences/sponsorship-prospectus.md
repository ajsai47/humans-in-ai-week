# Flagship Sponsorship Prospectus

Sponsorship tiers for HAIW flagship conferences in hub cities. These are separate from (and larger than) the global HAIW sponsorship tiers in the [Global Partnership Proposal](../global/global-partnership-proposal.md).

---

## Why sponsor a flagship?

- **1,000–2,000 attendees** — AI founders, engineers, researchers, investors, and enterprise leaders
- **Full-day visibility** — your brand in the room from 9 AM to 7 PM
- **AIC's partner network** — speakers from 100+ partner companies
- **National press** — flagships are the media hook for HAIW coverage
- **Recruitment pipeline** — direct access to AI talent in a major hub city

---

## Sponsorship tiers

### Title Sponsor — 1 per city

**Investment: $50,000–$100,000**

| Benefit | Included |
|---------|----------|
| "Presented by [Sponsor]" on all materials | Yes |
| Logo: event page, banners, stage backdrop, badges, email headers | Top placement |
| Keynote or fireside chat slot (10–15 min) | Yes |
| Branded breakout session or workshop | Yes |
| Exhibition booth (premium location) | Yes — largest booth |
| Tickets | 20 VIP + 10 general |
| VIP dinner seats | 4 |
| Newsletter feature (200K+ subscribers) | Dedicated paragraph |
| Press release inclusion | Named as Title Sponsor |
| Post-event impact report | Custom report |
| Logo on HAIW global website | Featured |
| Social media | Co-branded posts, tagged in all event content |

**Investment: $15,000–$35,000**

| Benefit | Included |
